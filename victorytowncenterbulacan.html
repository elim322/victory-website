Victory Town Center - San Jose Bulacan

Victory Town Center is located at the endpoint of San Jose Del Monte Public Transportation Network. Strategically located at the Northeast periphery of Metro Manila, West of Marilao & Sta. Maria and North of Norzagaray.

The mall has 8,680sqm gross floor areas including the 6,200sqm of retail spaces.

Features:

  • 100 Micro-Retail spaces also known as "Tiangge"
  • Puregold Jr. Supermarket
  • Uno Department Store
  • Mang Inasal, Goldilocks
  • DIY Shop Hardware, Acebedo Optical, CompuClick
  • Generika Drugstore
  • Validosa Salon, Photography Studio
  • Cebuana Lhuillier, Tambunting Pawnshop, Western Union
  • Entertainment & Leisure Center
  • Bingo

    • Transport Hub
