Victory Town Center - Lemery, Batangas

Victory Town Center Lemery was the first shopping center situated at the Municipality of Lemery, Batangas.First provincial branch of Victory Malls established on 2010

GLA: 15,245 sqm.
Retail Space: 11,417 sqm.
Retail Shops: 114
Parking Slots: 100
- With major anchor store - Citimart Supermarket; a one-stop shop wherein the Supermarket is located at the Ground floor and the Department Store on the Second level


Features:

  • Citimart Supermarket
  • FC Home Center
  • Bingo Boutique
  • Hendison Shoe Plaza
  • Tambunting Pawnshop

    • Transport Hub

    Established along Atienza corner Rizal Streets, Barangay District II.
    Tricycle Route: Lemery Poblacion, Taal, San Luis, Agoncillo, San Nicolas, C5;
