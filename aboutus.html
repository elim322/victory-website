The Mall
Victorymall is set to inject new excitement into the retail, dining and entertainment scene.

The Owner
Guru Property Development and Management Corporation is a company engages in development, management, operation and construction of different commercial buildings and mall operations.
We aim to provide not only diversified commercial and mall management operations in the cities and provinces, but also construction, operations and management of wet and dry markets including public markets.

Our Vision
To be nation's provider in mall management and operations committed to provide affordable spaces for the retailers and convenient shopping experience.

Our Mission
To provide quality complete business mix that would cater to various market. To deliver and maintain quality service to tenants. To locate small enterpreneurs and ordinary people in one shopping destination, where affordability and convenience unite.
