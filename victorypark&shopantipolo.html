Victory Park & Shop

Victory Park and Shop is a 5-level Shopping Mall with 2 basement parking area. Our mall is situated right beside the Antipolo Cathedral- Our Lady of Peace and Good Voyage Shrine. It is strategically located near schools, public markets, transportation terminals, subdivisions and other establishment.

The mall has 12,513 sqm gross floor areas including the 7,035.23 sqm of retail spaces. Victory Park & Shop holds 166 leasable spaces with different shops, restaurants, kiosks and food carts.

Features:

  • Micro-retail spaces also known as "Tiangge"
  • PureGold Jr. Supermarket
  • McDonald's, Razon's of Guagua, The Original Savory, Tokyo Tokyo, Starbucks
  • Baker's Fair
  • DIY Hardware
  • National Bookstore, BPI Bank, CDR-King
  • RRJ/Lee, Bizaare, Bum, Girlshoppe, The Look
  • HBC, Haircut Encarnacion Salon, Acebedo Optical
  • Bayad Center, Tambunting, Cebuanna Lhuiller, Cebu    Pacific & 2Go
  • Amusement Centre
