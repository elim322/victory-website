Victory Uptown Market - Tarlac

Victory Uptown Market is mostly a haven of fresh-produced farm and poultry animal and variety of marine products and its by-products, as well as locally and regionally grown vegetable products with major grocery and food amenities. It also features a shopping depot of house ware and dry good products.

GLA: 2,900 sqm.
Retail Space: 11,417 sqm.
Building Facilities: 10 major building with specialized stall types per merchandise mix.
Parking Slots: Full capacity of 80 cars
Business Section: Wet Market, Rice, Commercial, Vegetable Bagsakan, Homeware Depot
