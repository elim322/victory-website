Victory Pasay Mall

The former mall was known as Pasay City Mall & Market and now known as VICTORY PASAY MALL.

Victory Pasay Mall is a 3 level commercial building with 100 parking slots. The mall has 10,482 gross floor areas including the 6,758.21sqm of retail spaces. The Mall is directly linked to the Libertad LRT Line 1 Station,and situated right in front of Pasay Public Market.

Features:

  • 100 Micro-Retail Hub also known as "Tiangge"
  • PureGold Jr Supermarket
  • Mart1 Department Store
  • KFC, Greenwich, Mang Inasal, Happy Orange, Razon's Halo-Halo
  • Tokyo Tokyo, Pub Express
  • Red Ribbon, Baker's Fair
  • Haircut Encarnacion Salon, Xtensions
  • Expressions, PC Broker, Black Cat Nails & Footcare Salon
  • LBC, Tambunting Pawnshop, Cebuana Lhuiller, Security Bank
  • Club Synergy
  • Bingo Boutique

    • Transport Hub

    The jeepneys and the existing light rail system or LRT 1 are considered the primary mode of transportation in Victory Pasay Mall. The jeepneys with longer Metro Manila routes mostly run along Taft Avenue coming from Manila bound for Baclaran & Vice versa passes the mall, same on Arnaiz Sreet formerly known as Libertad Street routes from Roxas Boulevard to Makati.
