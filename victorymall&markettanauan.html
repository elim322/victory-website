Victory Mall & Market - Tanauan, Batangas

Victory Mall & Market is classified as “ General Commercial District “ of the Central Business District ( CBD) and part of the Urban Core Zone. Re-development of the existing Tanauan City Public Market into a modern, safe, convenient business and environment friendly and efficiently –operated market and commercial center with public transport terminal along Mabini Avenue.

- With the growing population of Tanauan - 170,000.00 it is now viable option for Franchises, Food, Entertainment, Medical, Services and
   Apparel and other related products and services.
- Very accessible to commuters (jeepneys, tricycles, UV Express vans)
- It is estimated that more than 20,000 people traverse on the area on a daily basis
- The presence of 28 Tricycle Operator’s and Driver’s Association



Features:

- 4 storey structure , fully air-conditioned at the Ground & Second Floors
- 400 parking slots and Terminal private vehicles, public utility vehicles
- Wet & Dry Building shall be a 2 storey structure with 966 stalls
- Dry Section- 637 stalls will be categorized into :
   Services,Salons,RTWs,Bags,Shoes,Accessories,Tailoring,Grocery,Rice
- Wet Section- 448 stalls will be for Fish, Meat and Vegetable


Transport Hub

Established along Mabini Ave., Tanauan Batangas.
Jeepney Route: Lipa City-Tanauan City, Tanauan City-Talisay, San Pablo City-Tanauan City, Calamba City-Tanauan, Calamba City-Lipa City via Tanauan, Balibago (Sta. Rosa) –Tanauan City, Balibago (Sta. Rosa)-Tanauan via SLEX,S an Pablo City-Tanauan City;
